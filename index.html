Bank connections

Connect your accounts via Open Banking (GoCardless — free)

Connected banks
Copies all live balances into your offline data
How it works

1. Deploy the backend to Vercel (see setup guide) and paste the URL above.

2. Click + Connect bank, pick your bank from the list, and you'll be redirected to your bank's secure consent page.

3. After consenting, you'll be brought back here and your live balances will load automatically.

4. Use ⟳ Sync all any time to refresh balances. GoCardless fetches from your bank in real-time.

Bank connections expire after 90 days and will need re-authorising. GoCardless is free for personal use.

Import CSV

Import transactions or account data from a CSV file

ℹ
CSV format

Expected columns: name, type, amount, category. Category should be income, expense, or savings. For accounts: name, type, balance where type is bank/isa/lisa/pension/premium/credit/mortgage/loan.
